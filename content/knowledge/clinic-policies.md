# Clinic Policies and Concierge Support
Category: Operations
Tags: payment, financing, cancellations, front desk

Aurora Dental Atelier is built around a concierge-style patient experience. The front desk confirms benefits before restorative or cosmetic treatment, sends reminders by text, and leaves time for patients who want to talk through estimates in plain language.

We accept most PPO plans, HSA and FSA cards, and third-party financing for larger treatment plans. Patients without insurance receive a written estimate before care starts.

The clinic asks for 24 hours notice for schedule changes. Same-day changes are reviewed case by case, especially when the team is holding longer cosmetic or implant consult blocks.
