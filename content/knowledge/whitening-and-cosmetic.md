# Whitening and Cosmetic Planning
Category: Cosmetic
Tags: whitening, smile design, consultation, sensitivity

Cosmetic patients usually start with a whitening consult or a treatment-planning visit. The team checks sensitivity history, current restorations, and shade goals before recommending in-office whitening, take-home trays, or a broader smile refresh plan.

Patients who are considering whitening before veneers or restorative work are encouraged to mention that goal early so the dentists can sequence treatment correctly.
