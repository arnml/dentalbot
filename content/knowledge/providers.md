# Meet the Providers
Category: Team
Tags: mario, stefania, cosmetic, pediatric

Dr. Mario Bianchi leads restorative and cosmetic treatment planning. Patients usually choose Mario when they are exploring implant consults, smile design, or multi-step treatment plans and want a calm consultation with visuals and sequencing.

Dr. Stefania Costa focuses on preventive care, whitening, pediatric visits, and emergency access. Families often book with Stefania because her schedule includes reserved daytime blocks for children and urgent issues.
