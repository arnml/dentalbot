# Emergency Dental Care
Category: Clinical
Tags: urgent care, pain, swelling, chipped tooth

Aurora Dental Atelier keeps same-week emergency blocks open for tooth pain, swelling, broken restorations, and chipped front teeth. The goal is to reduce discomfort quickly and decide whether the patient needs immediate treatment or a follow-up visit.

Emergency appointments are typically 30 minutes and usually start with a focused exam, imaging when needed, and a short treatment discussion. Stefania usually handles first-line urgent visits unless the case is clearly restorative or implant-related.
