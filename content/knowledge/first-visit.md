# First Appointment Experience
Category: Patient journey
Tags: new patient, cleaning, x-rays, exam

New patients usually spend about 60 minutes in the office for a comprehensive exam and cleaning. The visit includes updated digital X-rays when clinically appropriate, periodontal charting, a cleaning or hygiene review, and time with the dentist to discuss priorities.

Mario tends to lead treatment-planning conversations for complex restorative cases. Stefania often sees families and pediatric patients who want a clear, efficient preventive visit with practical home-care advice.
